Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes, this website is free to use. You can access lessons, exercises, and quizzes without any cost.
3. Do I need to create an account?
No, you do not need to create an account to start learning. However, creating an account may help you track your progress.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
You can build your English vocabulary by practicing daily lessons, completing quizzes, learning new words, and using them in sentences.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Yes, we offer certificates after successfully completing certain courses. You may need to complete all lessons and pass the final assessment to receive a certificate.
